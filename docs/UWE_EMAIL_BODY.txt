───────────────────────────

Lot 237 has TWO legitimate offerings at two different price points:

┌─────────────────────┬──────────────┬────────────────────────────────┐
│ Option              │ Price        │ What's Included                │
├─────────────────────┼──────────────┼────────────────────────────────┤

│ House + Land        │ $1,066,400   │ Land + modular home + site work│
└─────────────────────┴──────────────┴────────────────────────────────┘

Both prices were correct in the database. The problem was they weren't clearly 
labeled in the UI, so it LOOKED like a mistake.


WHAT I FIXED
────────────

1. PUBLIC SITE MAP MODAL (when you hover on a lot)

   Before: Just two prices, no context
   After:  Clear tier labels with descriptions:

      🏠 HOUSE + LAND PACKAGE
         $1,066,400
         Includes land, modular home build & site works

      📍 SERVICED LAND ONLY
         $610,700
         Titled land only — build your own design


2. REGISTRATION FORM (when filling out interest)

   Before: Price options without clear labeling
   After:  Added tier badges [FULL PACKAGE] and [LAND ONLY]
