## 1. Three-minute live-demo script (Seafields / Branscombe ROI portal)

**(0:00–0:20 — the problem, in their language)**
"Every project you launch gets its own throwaway marketing site. Your agents chase buyers, but you
can't cleanly prove who introduced whom, and your lots sit while buyers wait on a builder. Three
separate problems. Watch what one system does with all three — this is live, our own estate."

**(0:20–1:20 — the attributed sell-through, on the real URLs)**
- Open `f2k-projects.vercel.app/branscombe-estate` → "Enriched estate page — interactive, real
  availability, not a brochure."
- The agent link `…/r/branscombe?ref=TOKEN` → lands on the waitlist → "A buyer arriving through an
  agent's link is locked to that agent at first touch — permanently, in the database. No more
  commission disputes."
- `/admin/roi-waitlist` → "The funnel: waitlist → qualification, who's attributed, who's unassigned,
  finance-ready, one-click 'send the full registration form', and a 48-hour auto-nudge so leads don't
  leak. CSV out to your CRM."

**(1:20–2:00 — the modular tie-in, the part they don't have)**
"And the homes on these lots aren't a separate problem — they're F2K modular house-and-land packages.
Seafields is land + modular home + this exact sell-through, running end to end. We turn raw lots into
turnkey, affordable, fast-to-settle homes."

**(2:00–3:00 — the frame + the ask)**
"We're not trying to be anyone's builder — we coordinate the modular supply and run the sell-through,
as a layer on top of your land. I want to run this as a paid pilot on **one** project for a developer
who's feeling this. Who in your network has lots moving and is fighting their sell-through or their
build delivery?"
